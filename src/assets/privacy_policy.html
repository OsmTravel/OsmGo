Osm Go! App Privacy Policy

We value your trust in using our Osm Go! application and are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your personal information:

Data Collection and Usage:

  • The Osm Go! app uses your device's location feature solely to display your real-time location on the map.
  • No location data is stored, recorded, or shared with any third parties.

Data Sharing:

  • Your location is never shared with other users, entities, or third-party services.
  • We do not transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Security:

We take appropriate security measures to safeguard your location data and ensure its confidentiality.

Privacy Policy Updates:

Any future updates to our privacy policy will be clearly communicated on our GitHub page to keep you informed.

Contact:

If you have questions about our privacy policy or need to reach out regarding concerns about your data, please feel free to open an issue on our GitHub repository under the dedicated issues section.

We appreciate your usage of Osm Go! and are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data throughout your usage experience.
